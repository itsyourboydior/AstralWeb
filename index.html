Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website?

Most business websites take 7-14 days. E-commerce sites take 14-21 days. We give you a timeline before starting.

How much does a website cost?

Business sites start at €300, e-commerce at €500. It depends on features, pages, and design complexity. We quote the total price upfront with no hidden fees.

Do you offer SEO services?

Yes! Every website comes with basic technical SEO included. We also offer advanced SEO packages for ranking on Google. Ask us about our SEO services.

Can I update the website myself?

Yes! We build easy-to-use websites. Most clients use WordPress for easy updates. We also offer ongoing maintenance if you prefer.

What information do you need from me?

After payment, you fill our detailed business questionnaire. We ask about your business, target audience, competitors, preferred colors/style, content, and goals. The more detail, the better your site!
