FAQ

Common Questions

Why shouldn't I just use Wix or Squarespace?

Website builders are incredibly slow, have terrible SEO capabilities, and you don't truly own your site. We build custom, high-performance sites that Google loves and actually convert visitors into customers.

How long does it take to build?

Most business websites take 7-14 days. E-commerce and more complex web apps take 14-21 days. We establish a clear timeline before any payment is made.

Will I be able to update the site myself?

Yes. We integrate easy-to-use Content Management Systems (CMS) so you can easily update text, add blog posts, or manage products without needing to code.

Do you offer ongoing support after launch?

Yes! We provide ongoing support and maintenance packages. Whether you need technical updates, content changes, or continuous SEO optimization, we've got you covered.

What happens if I don't like the initial design?

We work closely with you during the questionnaire phase to understand your vision. We provide iterations until you are 100% satisfied with the design before moving to final development.
